This group photo captures the( Navin Vidyarthi Bhetghat tatha Swagat Samaroh ) Gorkha Students Fresher's and Felicitation Program of Pandu College 2025. Organized by the Pandu College Gorkha Students Family ( Gorkha Yuva Sahityik Samaj ), the event was held on Friday, September 12th, 2025, starting at 9:00 AM in the S003 Room.The atmosphere is filled with joy and energy as students celebrate together, many wearing traditional attire and "Khada" to honor Gorkha culture. In the center, the newly crowned Mr. and Miss Fresher stand proudly among their peers, embodying the spirit of "Jai Gorkha." This celebration marks a significant milestone for the new batch, offering a powerful platform for building community and unity. It is more than just a welcome program; it is about students and their heritage working together to create a smarter, more connected future for the college family.
