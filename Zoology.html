About

The department of Zoology was established in 1963 with the initiative of founder principal Late S. K. Sen, an eminent educationist. At the beginning there were 23 students in the enrollment of the first batch. The ‘Honours Course’in Zoology was introduced in the session 1980-81. This course was later renamed as ‘Major Course’ when the three years Degree Course was introduced in the +2 pattern (part I + part II). Again the pattern of this course was changed into the pattern of TDC (1+1+1) in 2003 by Gauhati University in accordance with the recommendation of UGC. Semester system was introduced by Gauhati University in 2011.
PG course was introduced in the Department of Zoology of Pandu College in the session 2013-14 with a view to ushering higher education in this region of Assam. There are three special papers viz. ‘Entomology’, ‘Fish Biology &Fishery Science’ and 'Ecology and Wildlife Biology' in MSc fourth semester. Special paper 'Ecology and Wildlife Biology' was introduced in 2022-23.
From the very beginning the department has been producing excellent result. Many students who passed out of this department have already occupied attractive positions in different governmental, non-governmental institutions and organisations.
Vision:
Department of Zoology, Pandu College stands strong to reach excellence by generating fruitful social, economic, cultural human resources with ecological coexistence through promotion of quality education.
Mission:
To promote holistic development by ‘redefining zoology’ to students of every age so that they develop interest in science.
To develop intellectual, communicative and social skill.
To strive for quality in teaching, learning and research that has relevance to the society and employability.
To endorse academic excellence.
To cultivate critical thinking that can spark creativity and innovation.
To transform lives and serve the society by encouraging participation of rural students in higher education
