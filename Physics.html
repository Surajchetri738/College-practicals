About

The department of Physics is one of the oldest departments of the college. The department was established on 01.08.1963 with Prof. Nirupam Guha as the founder head and Sri Niranjan Dey as the bearer. The pre-university courses were introduced in the year 1963. The Department has flourished to its present form under the strong guidance of Prof. Bijan Bhadury. The degree classes were started from the year 1966 in pass course only and honours courses were introduced in 1972. At present, the department possesses talented and experienced faculty members who are energetic and student friendly. Apart from the regular activities, this department has been indulged in giving a strong base to the students through certain interactions including imparting value based education, career related opportunities, exposure to the world wise scenario etc. The department organizes seminar, popular talk etc. by inviting eminent scholars to share their knowledge with the students. Also departmental seminars are regularly arranged among the students. Students are encouraged to participate in various competitions like poster exhibition, essay writing, workshop, seminar etc. organized by different institutes. The department has been quite successful in moulding a good number of well-established citizens across the country and abroad.
