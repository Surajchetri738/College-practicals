This is the official event banner for CodeFest, organized by the Department of Computer Science and Application at Pandu College. The event was held in collaboration with the Incubation Centre and the Institution’s Innovation Council. The central theme of the event is "Let's talk about the future," and it was scheduled for January 31st, 2026, at 10 a.m. The venue for this gathering was the Digital Classroom located on the 2nd floor of the Arts Block. This event represents a powerful platform for exploring how technology is changing our way of thinking and creating. It highlights the importance of humans and technology working together to build a smarter, more innovative future through coding and digital advancement.
