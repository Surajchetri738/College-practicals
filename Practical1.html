About

The Department of Computer Science and Application at Pandu College was opened in the year 2000 with the help of financial grant from UGC to start a vocational subject called Computer Application. The Department offers Degree Course on Bachelor Computer Applications (BCA) and BSc Computer Science Major under Gauhati University and Two Year Higher Secondary course on Computer Science and Application under Assam Higher Secondary Education Council.
The Department has a comprehensive curriculum covering Computer Hardware and Software with emphasis on practical learning. It has well equipped labs with modern hardware, 20 desktop PCs connected through LAN and smart board for digital classes.
