About

Vision
To grow the department as a center of excellence in all aspects of education, research, and development in chemical sciences.
Mission
To deliver excellent undergraduate chemistry teaching that prepares students for successful careers with social sensitivity and actively promotes the culture of research amongst students and faculty.
The Chemistry Department is one of the pioneering Departments of the college. Besides enriching with experienced faculty members, the department rapidly develops its vigor and vitality from its strong Alumni base. It offers undergraduate courses and imparts education in many innovative ways.
